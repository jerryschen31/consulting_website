My Services

Bioinformatics Services

With 20 years of experience as a Ph.D. in Bioinformatics and a former Head of Bioinformatics and Software, I can help with any aspect of your bioinformatics infrastructure and workflow. My services include:

Bioinformatics Pipelines

Tired of dealing with messy code and complex workflows? I can help build easy-to-use, scalable, reproducible pipelines so that you can turn your raw sequencing data into results, fast.

Cloud Infrastructure

I am an expert with AWS cloud services, and can setup, maintain and troubleshoot your AWS cloud infrastructure for bioinformatics, using the most efficient, cost-saving resources.

Data Analysis

Need help with finding significant mutations in your tumor samples, or discovering differentially expressed genes between your sample cohorts? I can help with sequencing QC, genome alignment, differential expression, gene ontology analysis, single cell analysis, variant calling and much more.

Genomic Databases

Need help building a gene expression, variant, or some other genomic database for your samples? With expertise in relational (SQL) and non-relational (NoSQL) databases, I can design your tables and schema, and write automated scripts to handle database import and management of all of your data.
